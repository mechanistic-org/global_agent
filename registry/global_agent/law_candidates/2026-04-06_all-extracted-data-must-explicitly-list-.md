# LAW CANDIDATE — 2026-04-06

**Rule:** All extracted data must explicitly list its sources in frontmatter to prevent hallucination and ensure provenance.

Every piece of extracted data, regardless of its source (e.g., from an ingested PDF), must explicitly list its `sources: []` in its frontmatter. This rule is critical for preventing hallucinated context and ensuring the integrity, traceability, and reliability of all information within the system.
