# LAW CANDIDATE — 2026-04-07

**Rule:** NanoClaw must operate ephemerally for each ingestion task.

NanoClaw's ingestion process must operate ephemerally. This means NanoClaw must boot, read the asset, process it, and then terminate, ensuring a clean, stateless, and efficient operation for each individual ingestion task.
