---
title: The unmaintained `nlm.exe` shim is being abandoned for a direct `notebooklm-py`
  library integration.
date: '2026-04-07'
context_node: conversation_miner
---

The legacy `nlm.exe` shim, previously used for NotebookLM interaction, has been identified as unmaintained and a "ghost." The decision has been made to abandon it in favor of directly importing the `notebooklm-py` Python library, ensuring a more stable and maintainable integration.
