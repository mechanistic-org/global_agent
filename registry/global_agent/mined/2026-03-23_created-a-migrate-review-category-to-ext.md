A dedicated 'MIGRATE-REVIEW' category was established for scripts identified as having potential 'gold' – agent-usable patterns, valuable integrations, or significant logic – requiring content review before migration. This process ensures that valuable insights and reusable code patterns are extracted and preserved, rather than being lost during cleanup.
