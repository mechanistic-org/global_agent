A specific workaround was established for Ishikawa diagrams: since native Mermaid lacks a 'fishbone' type, a custom generator will produce strictly formatted Markdown Mindmaps or Hierarchical lists in Mermaid. This ensures the visualization requirements are met despite tool limitations.
