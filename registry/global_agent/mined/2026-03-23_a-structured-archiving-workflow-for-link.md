---
title: A structured archiving workflow for LinkedIn comments ensures content lifecycle management and auditability.
date: 2026-03-23
context_node: conversation_miner
---

A specific workflow has been implemented for managing LinkedIn comments, involving the archiving of posted self-comments from a `drafts/comments/` directory to a `posted/comments/` directory. This structured approach ensures a clear audit trail for content, separates active drafts from published material, and streamlines the content lifecycle management process. It's a practical application of file-system based state management.
