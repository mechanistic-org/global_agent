title: Foundational logging, mapmaking, and provenance ensure inbound data remains organized and discoverable, preventing a 'junk drawer' scenario.
date: 2026-04-06
context_node: conversation_miner

To prevent inbound data from becoming disorganized, foundational structural enhancements are implemented, including strictly formatted logging to a global `timeline.md`, automatic registration of new topological nodes in a central `index.md`, and mandatory provenance tracking. These components act as 'the glue' for data integrity, discoverability, and system coherence.
