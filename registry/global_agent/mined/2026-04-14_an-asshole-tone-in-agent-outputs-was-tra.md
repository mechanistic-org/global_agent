---
title: An "asshole" tone in agent outputs was traced to aggressive prompting and persona bleed.
date: 2026-04-14
context_node: conversation_miner
---

A critical finding revealed that the "asshole" tone in agent-generated outputs, such as LinkedIn drafts and resumes, originated from overly aggressive prompting instructions and corrupted persona definitions. Specifically, directives for "brutalist, 'Hard Ore' bullets" and the "Hired Gun" persona bleeding into default settings led to pretentious incident-report formatting and a "know-it-all" voice. This highlights the profound impact of prompt and persona design on agent output.
