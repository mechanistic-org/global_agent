---
title: Aggressive prompting and persona definitions caused an "asshole" tone in agent outputs.
date: 2026-04-14
context_node: conversation_miner
---

The "asshole" tone in agent-generated LinkedIn drafts and resumes was identified as a critical issue, stemming from hyper-aggressive prompting instructions and corrupted persona definitions. Specifically, directives like "brutalist, 'Hard Ore' bullets" and the "Hired Gun" persona were found to be bleeding into default outputs, leading to pretentious and preachy content. This highlights a significant challenge in maintaining desired agent voice.
