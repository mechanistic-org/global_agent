---
title: Mandatory Frontmatter Schema implemented and enforced for LinkedIn posts, standardizing metadata.
date: 2026-04-07
context_node: conversation_miner
---

A mandatory Frontmatter Schema has been retroactively applied and enforced for all LinkedIn post files, standardizing metadata such as `title`, `pubDate`, `status`, `thread_id`, `arc_position`, and `post_url`. This ensures consistent content management and data integrity across all published posts, preventing orphaned drafts and inconsistent states.
