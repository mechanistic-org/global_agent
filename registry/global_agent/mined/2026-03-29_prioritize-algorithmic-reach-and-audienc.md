The primary strategic focus for LinkedIn content should remain on maximizing algorithmic reach and engaging the target senior, geographically concentrated audience, rather than solely optimizing for follower growth. The current 19x reach amplification and strong demographic signals are more valuable indicators of success than the slow follower conversion rate.
