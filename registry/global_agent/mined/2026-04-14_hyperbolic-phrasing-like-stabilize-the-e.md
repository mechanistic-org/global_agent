---
title: Hyperbolic phrasing like "stabilize the entropy" will be replaced with grounded, professional language.
date: 2026-04-14
context_node: conversation_miner
---

Hyperbolic phrasing, exemplified by "I stabilize the entropy of product development: structure the chaos, index the decisions, ship the hardware," was identified as an anti-pattern in agent-generated content. This type of language will be trimmed and replaced with simpler, more grounded expressions like "I lead complex hardware architectures from prototype to high-yield production." This change aims to enhance professionalism and clarity in agent communications.
