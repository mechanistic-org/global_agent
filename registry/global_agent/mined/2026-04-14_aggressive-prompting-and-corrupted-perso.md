---
title: Aggressive prompting and corrupted personas caused the 'asshole' tone in agent outputs.
date: 2026-04-14
context_node: conversation_miner
---

The root cause of the 'asshole' tone in agent-generated outputs, particularly in LinkedIn drafts and resumes, was identified as stemming from hyper-aggressive prompting instructions and corrupted persona definitions. This diagnostic breakthrough led to a comprehensive plan to refactor the agentic tone.
