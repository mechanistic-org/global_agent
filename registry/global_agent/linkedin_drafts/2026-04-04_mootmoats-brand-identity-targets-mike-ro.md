MootMoat's messaging is intentionally crafted to resonate with "Mike Rowe types"—technically capable individuals who build in the physical world (hardware, mechanical, manufacturing). This framing emphasizes an empowering DIY ethos, aiming to connect with a specific audience of physical engineers.
