The EN-OS engine now boasts an 'Always-On' architecture, enabling it to boot silently and automatically without requiring any human intervention. This significant enhancement was achieved by integrating PM2 as an enterprise-grade process manager, which orchestrates critical background processes like `enos-router` and `ollama-keepalive` for seamless operation.
