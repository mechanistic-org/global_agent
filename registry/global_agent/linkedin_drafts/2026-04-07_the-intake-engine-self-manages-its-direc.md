A key design principle for the Intake Engine was to prioritize ease of use and self-management. The scripts automatically construct their necessary tracking directories, `inbox` and `inbox/archive`, directly off the repository root. This allows users to simply drop files in, run the script, and the system autonomously structures the intelligence without any manual setup, offering a truly 'drop-and-run' experience.
