Modern Applicant Tracking Systems possess an implicit bias against physical hardware engineering. Their ontologies fail to capture or quantify critical realities like thermal resistance budgets, EMI mitigation, or DFM tradeoffs, rendering the actual expertise of hardware and mechanical engineers invisible.
