The Intake Engine, also known as 'Brains in Boxes', has been successfully localized and built, marking a significant milestone in autonomous knowledge capture. Users can now effortlessly drop unstructured files, including NotebookLM audio outputs, into the designated `inbox` directory, and the system autonomously parses and processes them, streamlining intelligence gathering.
