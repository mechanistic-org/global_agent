The EN-OS team has successfully implemented the sovereign asset ingestion pipeline, a significant breakthrough enabling agents to seamlessly parse diverse Office formats (PDF, Word, PowerPoint, Excel, CSV, Email) directly into LLM-optimized Markdown. This achievement completely bypasses cloud APIs, reinforcing data sovereignty and greatly expanding agent capabilities for processing unstructured data.
