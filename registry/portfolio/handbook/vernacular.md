# The Antigravity Lexicon: A Vernacular scrub 🏛️📖

To keep our communications high-signal, here is the official breakdown of the terms we are using:

### 1. The Hub (The Destination)

- **Hyperspace (The "Scrolly"):** The immersive, scroll-triggered narrative engine. It uses a split-screen layout where visuals and data "stick" while the narrative flows (e.g., `dreamjob.mdx`).
- **DataSheet (The "Classic"):** The structured, grid-based technical log. It looks like a high-density engineer's dossier (the current default for C|24).
- **The Work Realm:** The segment of the homepage that houses the project swarm and the "Fiche Strip" (where you select projects).

### 2. The Bridge (The Workflow)

- **Operation: PST Ore:** The process of mining legacy email archives for technical "Signal."
- **The Kiln:** The extraction/refinement pipeline where raw data (PSTs/JPGs) is purified into a Master Briefing for the AI.
- **Context Bridge:** The logic-handoff between **NotebookLM** (The Chief of Staff / Grounding Engine) and **Antigravity** (The Architect / Executing Agent).

### 3. The Evidence (The Grounding)

- **Evidence Locker:** The "Proof of Work" section at the footer of project pages, housing source documents, citations, and vendor reports.
- **Evidence Badge:** Small, verifiable UI tags (e.g., `[Doc #12]`) placed next to metrics in the text to prove they aren't halluncinated.
- **Intelligence Density:** The ratio of verifiable facts vs. marketing fluff. We are aiming for Level 10 Density.
