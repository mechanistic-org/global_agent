The Pre-CAD-Freeze Gate
No steel may be cut
until all 5 are resolved.
The EN-OS operates the same way. No agent commits until the constraint gates are cleared. Same discipline. Different substrate.
B-01
Shrinkage Factors
Apply 0.5–0.7% PC/Tritan shrink rate to master CAD. Without this, every cooled part will be oversized.
REQ-5.1
B-02
Material & Color Lock
Finalize resins. Execute custom elastomer compounding for Hyphen-Green (#4FE6B4) with UV stability validation.
REQ-5.2
B-03
Hopper Geometry Mapping
Resolve dimensional variances between 1/6 and 1/3 hopper ends/sidewalls to prevent binding.
REQ-5.3
B-04
Stacking System Lock
Final sign-off on 40mm pitch, hub registration, and geometric placement of the 4 elastomer feed points.
REQ-5.4
B-05
CAD Tolerance Sync
Final tolerance stack-up analysis of the ~459g V5 model. PRD must match physical CAD reality exactly.
